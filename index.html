About Us

Fudar Wheels is a pioneering electric vehicle (EV) fleet management company operating in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities across India. We specialize in sustainable and efficient transportation solutions, catering to the growing demands of e-commerce, logistics, and corporate mobility.

Our fleet of electric vehicles is dedicated to eco-friendly last-mile delivery and cab services, ensuring cost-effective, reliable, and environmentally responsible transport options.

At Fudar Wheels, we go beyond conventional fleet management by integrating advanced safety features. Our mission is to revolutionize urban mobility in underserved regions while contributing to a greener future.

With a growing team of gig workers and a fleet of EVs, we are committed to driving innovation and sustainability in Indias transportation sector.

Founder's Talk

The shift towards sustainable mobility is gaining momentum in India, but a significant gap remains in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities where electric vehicle (EV) adoption is still in its infancy. At Fudar , we recognized this challenge and saw an opportunity to bring cutting-edge EV fleet management to these underserved regions.

Fudar was founded with a vision to revolutionize last-mile delivery and urban transportation through an efficient and sustainable EV fleet. We identified a crucial need in smaller cities, where logistics and transportation businesses struggle with high fuel costs and unreliable delivery networks. Our mission is to provide cost-effective and eco-friendly solutions while ensuring seamless operations for businesses and commuters alike.

Currently, Fudar Wheels manages a fleet of electric vehicles, primarily focusing on logistics and last-mile deliveries. We have partnered with Flipkart and Big Basket to facilitate efficient, green deliveries, and our vehicles are operated by gig workers who benefit from flexible work opportunities.

Operating an EV fleet in smaller cities comes with its own set of challengescharging infrastructure, range anxiety, and driver training being the major ones. However, we see these as opportunities to innovate and create infrastructure that benefits both businesses and individuals. By working closely with e-commerce, logistics, and BPO companies, we aim to build a sustainable ecosystem that makes electric mobility accessible and reliable.
